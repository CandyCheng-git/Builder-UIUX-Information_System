Frequently Asked Questions

How many garden beds can the system support?

The HarvestPro WaterSense can manage up to 50 individual garden beds with independent monitoring and irrigation control for each bed.

What happens if my WiFi goes down?

The system has built-in offline mode that continues watering based on the last scheduled program. You'll receive notifications once connectivity is restored.

How accurate are the soil moisture sensors?

Our sensors provide Â±3% accuracy for soil moisture readings and automatically calibrate for different soil types and conditions.

Can I use this system with my existing irrigation setup?

Yes! The system is designed to integrate with most standard 1/2" and 3/4" irrigation lines and can control existing zone valves.

What's included in the warranty?

The 3-year warranty covers all hardware components, sensors, and valves. Software support and updates are included for the lifetime of the product.
